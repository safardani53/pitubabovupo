Tom Jones 2022 UK Tour: Tickets on sale now for summer shows

March 2024 · 3 minute read

TOM Jones tickets go on sale this morning and here's how you can get your hands on a pair.

The Welsh legend added two more dates to his tour this week, and will be performing at Longleat and Blickering Estate later this summer.

The Welsh singing sensation has announced two shows this summer, at Longleat Estate and and Blickering Estate and tickets will go on sale this morning.
