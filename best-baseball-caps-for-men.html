Best Baseball Caps For Men 2019 | The Sun UK

April 2024 · 4 minute read

WHICH are the best baseball caps to buy? With a history spanning over 150 years and so many brands and labels offering them, there are plenty to choose from.

We’ve selected the best baseball caps in this guide to help you decide. From sporty styles to sophisticated streetwear, there’s a design for you.

Baseball caps are loved by celebrities trying to go incognito - but they often manage to look good in the process too.

Ben Affleck’s cap sports a bright emblem on the front that livens up his otherwise low-key outfit, for example.

Meanwhile Orlando Bloom has chosen one in a colour that matches his jumper and jeans, keeping up an understated appearance.

A$AP Rocky wears his not to hide but to stand out - his has a contrast front and printed logo to match the bold style of the rest of his look.

Find the baseball cap to suit your style in the following guide to the best around today.

1. Most iconic baseball cap

The New York Yankees have one of the most recognisable emblems in the sporting world, and the typographic logo adds a sporty edge to this New Era baseball cap.

It’s constructed from a durable technical fabric in a classic six-panel design, and comes with a slidable adjuster at the back for a totally personalised fit that ensures maximum comfort.

  • New Era Yankees Black Cap, £17 from Urban Outfitters - buy here

2. Best graphic baseball cap

As the baseball cap moves constantly further away from its sporting roots, new styles come up all the time.

This River Island cap has an embroidered rose at the front, bringing graphic impact for a really modern look.

Team it with a plain T-shirt and sweatpants for a city-ready outfit on a sunny day.

  • Beige Rose Baseball Cap, £7 from River Island - buy here

3. Best trucker cap

Trucker-style baseball caps have mesh backs, and this New Era is a perfect option for an everyday look.

This design will look great worn forwards or backwards and teamed with a plaid shirt and jeans.

  • New Era Ne Lge Ess Trucker La Dodgers Unisex Caps, £14.99 from Foot Loker - buy here

4. Best logo baseball cap

This snapback design from adidas comes in cool monochrome and features the brand’s iconic three-stripe emblem - a powerful graphic combination.

It has a panelled construction and air vents at the top to keep you cool in the sun.

Wear it with equally impactful sporty pieces to create a streetwear-inspired look.

  • Adidas The Packcap, £22 from JD Sports - buy here

5. Best vintage-style baseball cap

Designed to look like a vintage find, this Urban Outfitters baseball cap is made from washed cotton and has the Coca-Cola logo embroidered across the front.

The metal clasp at the adjustable back adds an authentic touch, while the light colour and top air vents will keep you cool on hot days out.

  • UO Coca-Cola Beige Cap, £28 from Urban Outfitters - buy here

6. Best suede baseball cap

Baseball caps aren’t all about practicality - check out this design from Ralph Lauren in supple suede.

It’s a particularly sophisticated take on the look, and has been responsibly crafted, using heritage techniques featuring signature chain-stitched 'RRL'. 

Use this design on bright-but-cold days or particularly stylish occasions.

  • Ralph Lauren Suede Ball Cap, £170 from Ralph Lauren - buy here
