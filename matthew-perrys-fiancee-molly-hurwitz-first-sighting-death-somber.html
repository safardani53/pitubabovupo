MATTHEW Perry's ex-fiancee Molly Hurwitz has been pictured for the first time since Perry was found dead in a hot tub at his Los Angeles home on Saturday.

Hurwitz,32 looked downcast as she walked her dog through a Los Angeles park near her home and spoke somberly with a group of friends.

The agent and talent manager wore black leggings and a black and white plaid flannel shirt knotted at her waist over a white tee.

She hid her eyes behind dark sunglasses and a black hat as news of Perry's death continued to sink in for the Friend star's family and colleagues.

At one point the agent was seen crying.

Perry and Hurwitz began dating in 2018 and got engaged in November 2020 before calling off the relationship in June 2021.

The Death of Mathew Perry

Perry was found dead in his jacuzzi on Saturday, where he is believed to have drowned.

Sources from law enforcement originally disclosed that they were called to the residents for a cardiac arrest.

 Police initially said that no drugs were found at the scene and no foul play was suspected.

The outlet revealed that prior to the tragedy, Perry came home after a two-hour game of pickleball, and sent his assistant out on an errand shortly after.

Pickleball is a racket game played on a court a quarter of the size of a tennis court, with a lower net.

The game has rocketed in popularity after a number of Hollywood celebrities started promoting it during lockdown.

The assistant is said to have returned about two hours later, where he discovered Perry unresponsive and reached out to emergency services.
