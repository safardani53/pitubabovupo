All hail the ultra-fit, supertoned celebrity body! It’s tough enough staying svelte in your 20s, 30s and 40s, but plenty of fitspirational stars prove age really is just a number. Although Brooke Shields has acted for decades and is still glowing, she hasn’t always felt secure, she revealed to Yahoo in 2019.

“I had a very strong disconnect to my body,” the former Suddenly Susan star said at the time. “I was the face on the covers, I was the eyebrows or the face or whatever the thing was that people and the press and everybody focused on. And because I was never really skinny, I never did runway. So I was always the one that was doing the magazines but often never fit into the samples.”

She also was confused about being labeled as a “sex symbol” by others.

“It was said that I was the most famous virgin, which is really great to live with,” she quipped. “How do you justify sexy screen siren and famous virgin? It’s a real disconnect and it’s really hard. And so, I look back and I think, I’m still surviving and I can’t believe that I didn’t turn into a train wreck. Seriously.”

She also revealed to PorterEdit that she was body-shamed by her own family, something that really hurt her confidence.
