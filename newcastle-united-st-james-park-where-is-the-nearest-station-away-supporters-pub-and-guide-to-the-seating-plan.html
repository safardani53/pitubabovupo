Newcastle United St James' Park - Where is the nearest station, away supporters pub and guide to the

August 2024 · 2 minute read

IT'S well known for its fiery atmosphere, and a trip to Newcastle's St James' Park will leave you in no doubt that the Geordie's are a passionate lot.

The turf has been graced by the likes of club legends such as Malcolm McDonald, Peter Beardsley and of course Alan Shearer (there's even a statues of the latter).

Here, Sunsport gets the lowdown from the fans on the key things to know to ensure you have a great day (and night) out at the football.

 

What is the capacity?

52, 354

Club nickname?

The Magpies

Where is the nearest away pub?

A head of steam (opposite train station)

How much for a pie?

£3.20 (chicken Balti pie)

Where is the nearest train station?

Newcastle Central Railway Station - 1/2 mile

Any decent nightclubs to go afterwards?

Floritas or house of smith. Could head towards the gate too.

What's the best view at the ground?

The Milburn stand

Average adult ticket - £450

What is so special about the place?

The latest Shearer statue that caused a fuss
The Sir Bobby Robson statue
The stadium being in the heart of the city makes it pretty special too

How old is the stadium?

1892
