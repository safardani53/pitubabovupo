Chrissy Teigen Pumps Breast Milk in a Car: Photo

August 2024 · 2 minute read

This is what parenthood looks like. John Legend and Chrissy Teigen were on their way to a restaurant when the 32-year-old whipped out her breast pump and got to work. An impressed Legend snapped a photo of the moment and shared it on Instagram. 

“I know it’s Father’s Day and all but my wife is pretty awesome,” he wrote on Sunday, June 17. “She’s taking me to dinner but still on mommy duty.” 

The couple welcomed their son, Miles, on May 16. The baby boy, who is now 4-weeks-old, joined big sister Luna, 2.  

Teigen celebrated her husband of four years on Sunday with a family brunch and paid tribute to him in an Instagram post that showed him and Luna cuddled up on a couch. “What a man, what a man, what a mighty good man,” began the cookbook author, referencing the 1994 Salt-N-Pepa song. “Thank you for the beautiful life and these beautiful lives you have given me. My best friend and partner always, the most loving and generous and kind human in the world. And the smartest. I didn’t say funniest so you know I’m not lying here. We love you with all our hearts. Happy Father’s Day.”
