Sharing an image of the pair in their 1992 movie A League Of Their Own, she wrote “Mess with my girl Rosie and you're messing with me!!! Cruelty never made anyone a winner. ❤️ #rebelhearts4ever”.

The star joins pal Katy Perry in getting “nude for Hillary”.
