Beyonce’s ‘Drunk In Love’

In the new video — one of the 17 she released on Dec. 13, Beyonce looks super sexy on the beach twerking and dancing in a scandalous bikini. Naturally, her hubby is right there too!

[hl_youtube src=”https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LTGMU1lf_fY” link=”https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LTGMU1lf_fY text=”Beyonce – Drunk In Love (Official Video) ft Jay-Z”]

However, it kind of reminds us of a song that Rihanna would do. Not only does it have a ridiculously close title to RiRi’s hit, but the lyrics and the video are super sexual, much like many by the Barbados singer (you’ve seen “Pour It Up” right?).

Rihanna and Beyonce have never really been close, and there’s been a great deal of beef between them. After Beyonce released “Bow Down,” Rihanna liked a quote on Instgram that said, “A lot of celebrities just want money, fame, power, fancy cars, houses all over the world and have people bow down to them. To me, that’s frightful behavior.”

She also posted a photo of a man in a speedo with “Beyonce” printed across the butt, with the caption, “Boys Love.” It referred to Drake‘s song “Girls Love Beyonce.” Is she saying that not all girls do?

Who knows — but read the lyrics to Bey’s new song below — it definitely sounds like a song Rihanna would sing!
