Molly-Mae Hague shows off 'Tommy' necklace after Tommy Fury leaves her in the UK for fight in the US

May 2024 · 2 minute read

MOLLY-Mae Hague is proudly wearing a necklace with boyfriend Tommy Fury's name on it after he left her in the UK for a fight in the US.

The boxer, 22, jetted off to America last week and is due to go head to head against MMA star Anthony Taylor in the ring this Sunday.

Molly, also 22, admitted she's unsure if she will be able to travel to the States for her boyfriend's American debut because of strict Covid-19 restrictions.

So the YouTuber has decided to show her support in another way - by wearing a Tommy necklace around her neck.

Molly took to Instagram to show off the stunning piece of jewellery on her Instagram Story.

She tagged her beau in the post and added a white love heart and diamond emoji next to his username.

Tommy, who is the younger brother of Tyson Fury, began boxing aged 12 and started his professional journey as a light-heavyweight seven years later.

Tommy's fight this Sunday is on the undercard of online rival Jake Paul vs Tyron Woodley.

It will give him the perfect chance to send out a message to Paul, who headlines the night against Tyron Woodley.

The two clashed after Paul shared a 'screenshot' of an alleged message from Molly to him, asking him to show her around America.

Molly and Tommy met back in 2019 on the fifth season of Love Island.

The pocket rocket caught the eye of Tommy immediately after she entered the villa as a bombshell.

Although they were involved in a love triangle alongside Molly's best pal Maura Higgins, Tommy made it clear that he only had eyes for one girl.

Since shooting to fame two years ago, the pair have been inseparable and ticked off many mile stones in their relationship.

They moved into their stunning £1.3m Cheshire home last year, and have spoken openly about marriage and children in the past.

Over the past two years Molly has always shown huge love and support towards her boyfriend's job.

However, she has admitted in the past that it's "horrendous" to watch him fighting from the sofa.

She told her 5.9m followers on Instagram: "I try and play it cool but I can't explain how horrendous it is watching your boyfriend box from home. Glad that's over!!! Until next time."
