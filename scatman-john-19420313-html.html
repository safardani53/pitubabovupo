John Paul Larkin, known by his stage name Scatman John, is an American musician and singer who created a combination of song and dance music, best known for his 1995 hits "Scatman" and " Scatman's World ". He is also a pianist and jazz singer. Scatman John has sold millions of records worldwide and was named Best New Artist at the Echo Awards in Germany. He received the Annie Glenn Award from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association for excelling in the stuttering community and the National Stutter Association's Hall of Fame.
