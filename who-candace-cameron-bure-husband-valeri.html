Who is Candace Cameron Bure's husband, Valeri Bure?

March 2024 · 2 minute read

CANDACE Cameron Bure rose to fame in the 1990s as the loveable D.J. Tanner on Full House.

Now, the actress is all grown up and has a family of her own, causing fans to wonder more about her husband, Valeri Bure.

Who is Candace Cameron Bure's husband, Valeri Bure?

Since 1996, Candace has been married to Valeri Vladimirovich "Val" Bure.

Val was born on June 13, 1974, in Moscow, Russia, and is known for his hockey career.

He first joined the NHL in 1992 after he was drafted by the Montreal Canadiens and went on to play 10 seasons before retiring.

Over the years, he was seen playing for Montreal, the Calgary Flames, Florida Panthers, St Louis Blues, and Dallas Stars.

Val was also a standout player for Russia's national team, helping them win a silver medal in 1998 and a bronze one in 2002.

Now, Val operates Bure Family Wines in St Helena, California with his wife.

How did Candace and Val meet?

Candace met her husband during Full House's final season thanks to her former co-star, Dave Coulier, who portrayed the role of Uncle Joey.

At the time, Dave, who is an avid hockey fan, was playing in a charity game with Val and invited the show's cast to watch.

It was while she was sitting in the stands, scoping out the guys with Lori Loughlin, that Candance first laid eyes on her future husband.

“We were looking at these two cute men on the ice, and I was like ‘I wanna meet that one, the blonde one,’ which was Val,” Candace revealed in 2014, via US Weekly.

“We went on a date the very next day, and in less than a year we were engaged."

In return, Val sent Dave a signed hockey stick but Candance joked that she probably hasn’t “thanked him enough.”

Do Candace and Val have children?

Since tying the knot, the Bures have welcomed three children.

Candace and Val are the parents of sons Maksim and Lev and daughter Natasha.

While the couple's sons tend to stay out of the spotlight, Natasha is following in her mother's footsteps by becoming an actress.

She landed her first gig in 2011 and has since starred in The Famer and the Belle: Saving Santaland, Home Sweet Home, and Don't Sweat the Small Stuff: The Kristin Carlson Story.

Natasha also starred in an episode of Fuller House in 2020.
