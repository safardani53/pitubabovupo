COUPLE'S THERAPY

Recently, Amy and TJ opened up about their relationship issues that led them into couple's therapy.

The former ABC broadcasters began dating late last year and admitted their relationship has been far from perfect.

On last Tuesday's episode of their new podcast, Amy & TJ, the couple revealed they recently endured a big fight that resulted in them sleeping apart.

TJ explained that it happened after tension built up between the pair over minor issues, but Amy dished on the specific moment that set her off.

"For me, what sent me over the edge - and we've had this conversation so many times about your sleeping habits.

"And you like to stay on the couch, you like to not go to bed, you like to stay up - you started to like, make a meal at midnight, and I just was frustrated. I was like, 'Can we please just go to bed?'" the ex-TV star recalled.

Amy further shared that she was "done" after their long day, and TJ's behavior brought on their argument.

The duo also shared that having hiccups in their relationship is tough when they live and work together, leaving them with little space to get away.

However, Amy noted that the podcast co-hosts still have their own, separate apartments, despite recently teasing they secretly tied the knot.

"As we now relive this, we're now acknowledging, and I think a lot of couples, a lot of individuals have all dealt with holiday stress, how it affects you and your relationships, your family, your children. We're not at our best," Amy added.

For the rest of the episode, listeners heard Amy and TJ speak with their therapist, Dr. Jeff Gardere, for their first couple's therapy session after previously claiming they didn't need counseling.

Amy and TJ have stayed hush about their relationship since rumors surfaced that they were romantically involved last November.

At the time, they were legally married to other people - Amy to her now ex-husband, Andrew Shue, 56, and TJ to his now ex-wife, Marilee Fiebig, 46.

But the ex-TV hosts have been more vulnerable in recent weeks about their romance after launching their podcast.
