Celebrities In Festivals Hottest Looks Hollywood Life

February 2024 · 3 minute read

The hot temperatures in the California desert mean that sexy and revealing outfits are pretty much a staple at Coachella! At the 2022 event, Megan Thee Stallion certainly got the memo for her performance ensemble — channelling the 2000s with a revealing metallic lamé bodysuit for her performance!

Kendall Jenner is another celebrity who has worn quite a few sexy ensembles over the years, along with her younger sister Kylie Jenner. The cosmetics mogul and mom-of-tow has also looked pretty damn good at Coachella over the years and in 2017, she went to a party wearing a sparkling gold minidress, which showed off her toned legs, and also put her amazing figure on display.

Meanwhile, aside from the actual music festival, Coachella hosts a lot of sponsored events, including pool parties, so bikinis are a common occurrence. Olivia Culpo attended a 2022 pool party with a two-tone cream and rust colored bikini top with a mini skirt, channeling a cow girl! Keep reading for all the sexiest Coachella outfits, below.

Megan Thee Stallion

If there’s one thing Megan always serves, it’s a look. The Houston based rapper and ultimate “Hot Girl” didn’t disappoint with her first-ever Coachella appearance — turning heads in a super sexy metallic lamé bodysuit. The one piece look was covered in purple rhinestones and featured a flirty key-hole cutout in the center. Her performance also got the festival buzzing as she dropped new diss single “To Whom The F— It May Concern” alongside her major hits like “Savage” and “WAP.”

Kendall Jenner

Kendall rocked this beige, lace dress that featured side cutouts. Sure, it was a maxi dress that fell all the way down to her ankles, but the top portion of the dress left little to the imagination, as there were only thin pieces of fabric covering the model’s chest! Another year, she rocked a white, off-the-shoulder crop top and high-waisted skirt to the festival. The skirt’s double-slit put revealed major leg, while Kendall’s abs were also front and center in the look.

Olivia Culpo

Kicking off the 2022 Coachella Music Festival, Oliva dressed up as a sexy cowgirl for a party at the event. She rocked a brown and tan bikini top and a super chic mini skirt while carrying a rustic-looking handbag. The actress topped off the Western look with a pair of cowboy boots and a cowboy hat. Yee Haw!

Olivia looked stunning at a Revolve Party on April 13, 2019, when she wore a pair of skintight, high-waisted denim Bermuda shorts with cut-off, frayed hems. She styled the shorts with a super cropped black top that revealed a cheetah print bra underneath and she accessorized with peep-toe black heeled mules, a fanny pack, and sunglasses.

Olive turned up the heat a little more during the 2022 Coachella, as she dared to impress in a very sexy outfit. The former beauty pageant contestant slipped into a white bikini top, denim mini skirt and white cowboy boots for the desert getaway. She added a rainbow sweater to add a pop of color to the sultry ensemble.
