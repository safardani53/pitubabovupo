Elena Igorevna Radionova is a former Russian figure skater. She was the 2015 world bronze medalist, two European silver medals, two Grand Prix finalists, the 2017 Winter University champion, and the 2015 Russian national championship. At a junior level, she won two world championships at the grassroots level and the 2012-13 JGP final.
