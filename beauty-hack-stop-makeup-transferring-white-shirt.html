A MAKEUP ARTIST has shared her ultimate hack to stop foundation and fake tan from transferring onto the neckline of your tops. 

Rachel Nugent left people speechless by her hack - and it requires just one thing that you probably already have.

The 27-year-old was trying the trick out for the first time after seeing Olivia Attwood upload an Instagram story about it.

It involves spraying hairspray onto the collar and neckline of your white shirt to stop products transferring onto the fabric.

Rachel took to Tik Tok to test out the hack - sharing how well the hairspray worked as a barrier throughout the day - and left viewers impressed.

Many questioned how they had never heard about doing it before.

“It doesn’t feel sticky.

“I thought it would, but it was totally fine.
