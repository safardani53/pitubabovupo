Yves Mondesir, known as DJ Whoo Kid, is a Haitian American hip-hop DJ. He is currently signed to G-Unit Records and its affiliate, Shadyville Entertainment. He is a host of The Whoolywood Shuffle on Sirius / XM Radio Shade 45, in March 2009. His productions often use an audio clip to call out his DJ's name, with reverberation effects. He has two Billboard 200 albums, XXL Presents: Bad Season with Tech N9ne and The Whoodlum Ball with Smith and Hay and Ranna Royce.
