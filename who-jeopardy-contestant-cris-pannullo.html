Who is Jeopardy! contestant Cris Pannullo?

September 2024 · 2 minute read

OVER the years, Jeopardy! has seen countless contestants, but only a few have ever captured 21 consecutive wins.

Cris Pannullo added his name to that prestigious list after defeating Josh Gregor and Ilana Freedman on December 2, 2022.

Who is Jeopardy! contestant Cris Pannullo?

Cris Pannullo is a customer success operations manager from Ocean City, New Jersey, who started competing on Jeopardy! on September 30, 2022.

Since then, he has gone on to win 21 consecutive games and rack up a fortune along the way.

“Every single game you have two people just coming for you non-stop and the pressure doesn’t ever seem to stop,” Pannullo said after his 20th win, via Jeopardy.com.

“So, [winning] 20 games is incredible," he added.

Prior to Jeopardy! Pannullo competed as a professional poker player and was seen during a 2018 episode of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?, according to TV Insider.

How much has Cris Pannullo won?

Since Pannullo's Jeopardy! streak began, he has won over $740,000, which has put his name in the record books.

On December 6, 2022 Pannullo finally met a loss.

Fans were shocked after he missed a Daily Double and Final Jeopardy!

Those who have won more games than Pannullo in Jeopardy! history include Ken Jennings (74 games), Amy Schneider (40 games), Matt Amodio (38 games), James Holzhauer (32 games), and Mattea Roach (23 games).

In regards to his winnings, Pannullo previously stated that he plans on putting them under his mattress for a rainy day.

"In this economy, Ken, I’m just throwing it all under my mattress," he said, via TV Insider.

"The hard part is going to be getting it all flat, so I’m not laying on a lumpy mattress. But with some time, I’ll get it done."

How can I watch Jeopardy!?

Jeopardy!, hosted by Mayim Bialik and Jennings, can be watched live on weekdays starting at 7pm EST on your local NBC station.

The show can also be watched Saturdays at 7pm EST or streamed with Pluto TV.
