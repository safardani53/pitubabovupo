I DREAM of Jeannie star Barbara Eden has appeared to manage to hold on to her youthfulness.

Barbara, 92, seemingly cracked the code when it comes to aging gracefully-she was recently caught roaming around Los Angeles, California, with vitality.

Paparazzi captured snaps of Barbara walking to and entering her parked car.

The former TV star dressed casually, wearing a white crewneck sweatshirt, black pants, and sneakers.

She accessorized with a black crossbody bag, and sunglasses.

Sun shined down on Barbara, spotlighting her healthy glowing complexion and fit figure.

The sunlight also illuminated her signature thick bouncy blonde hair.

Nowadays, the icon tends to shy away from the limelight.

Barbara spends most of her days living a quiet life with her third husband, architect Jon Eicholtz, whom she married in 1991.

Celebrity photographers have rarely caught candids of Barabara out in public since her 92nd birthday in August.

PARTY GIRL

Barbara told People in August that for her birthday happening that month, she would have dinner with her husband and some friends at a steak house in Beverly Hills, California.

In addition to dinner, the mature blonde beauty invited her friends over to the home of the married couple.

Barbara and Jon reside in Beverly Hills with their 60-lb pet Labradoodle Bentley.

Instead of having a traditional birthday cake, Barbara had a lemon meringue pie in lieu of cake.

Prior to her birthday bash, Barbara appeared at a few events.

CONNECTING WITH FANS

Barbara attended the Hazard Fest Autograph Convention Show in October 2022 and the Rhode Island Comic Con in November 2022.

Connecting with fans is her ultimate goal when going to events like these, especially since many supporters travel to see her.

“They’re the best fans in the whole world,” Barbara told People.

"They’re very well-read. They’re good, nice people."

She added: "When I get up on the big stage, I speak, and then I do the Q&A, I’m sometimes surprised. They know more than I do about my work!”

Barbara actually discussed one particular fan with the news outlet.

“There’s one young man who comes quite often to the conventions,” Barbara continued.
