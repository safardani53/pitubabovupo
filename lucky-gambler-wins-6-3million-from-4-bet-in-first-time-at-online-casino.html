Lucky gambler wins 6.3MILLION from 4 bet in first time at online casino

July 2024 · 2 minute read

A LUCKY gambler has scooped £6.3 MILLION from a £4 bet on his first visit to an online casino.

Neil, from Aberdeen, walked away with the huge jackpot just an hour after making his first deposit with Casumo.

After putting £30 into his newly-opened account, he used £4 of it on a spin on the Hall Of Gods game.

Neil, who was playing the game on a laptop in his kitchen, was left speechless as he hit the multi-million windfall – the biggest win at Casumo to date.

It took him a while to realise he was a winner, even calling his wife in to check before getting excited.

But once the life-changing win had sunk in, the first people he called were his parents.

Neil said: “From my voice they knew something was up. I told them I’d won some money.

“Then I wrote down the entire win on single pieces of paper, laid them on the floor and when they came over I asked them to guess what it was.

“They thought it was a lottery number.”

Liverpool fan Neil says the first thing he plans to do with the money is buy himself a season ticket or a box to watch his favourite team at home.

He also wants to buy his parents a holiday home in Tenerife, take his children to Disney World and visit his sister in Australia.
