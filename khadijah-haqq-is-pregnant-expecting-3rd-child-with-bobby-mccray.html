Khadijah Haqq Is Pregnant, Expecting 3rd Child With Bobby McCray

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Another Dash Dolls darling! Pregnant Khadijah Haqq revealed that she and husband Bobby McCray are expecting baby No. 3.

“I’m pregnant!!!” the Side by Side star, 37, captioned her Thursday, August 27, Instagram post. “I couldn’t wait to share that my family is growing and we will soon welcome a new baby in our home.”

In the Instagram post, Haqq held out a positive pregnancy test with a smile.

“I’m so happy for you!!!!!!! Love you guys!!” Kim Kardashian commented on the social media upload, while Adrienne Bailon wrote, “You already know how happy my heart is for you! May the Lord continue to bless your beautiful family!!! Love you.”

Haqq and McCray, 39, tied the knot in July 2010. That same year, the couple welcomed their son, Christian, now 10. Celine, 6, arrived in 2014.

The Los Angeles native has been quarantining with her kids amid the coronavirus pandemic and shared an Instagram Story shot of their “homeschool” setup in April, writing that Celine “makes everything better.”

Haqq became an aunt in March when her twin sister, Malika Haqq, gave birth to her and her ex O.T. Genasis’ baby boy, Ace. “I’m never letting go nephew,” Khadijah captioned an Instagram photo at the time of the infant holding her finger. “Welcome to the family my little Panda.”

She went on to share a selfie with Malika and the newborn, writing, “You complete us.”
