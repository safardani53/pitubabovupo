How many kids does Offset have?

June 2024 · 3 minute read

MIGOS rapper Offset has four children with various baby mamas - including superstar Cardi B.

The rapper is a father to Jordan, Kody, Kalea, Kulture, and Wave Set Cephus.

Who is Offset?

Kiari Kendrell Cephus, a.k.a. Offset, 30, is an American rapper from Lawrenceville, Georgia.

He is best known for being in the family rap group Migos alongside Quavo and Takeoff.

Offset is the cousin of Quavo and Takeoff is Quavo's uncle.

They have amassed major fame as they have collaborated with Drake, 21 Savage and Cardi B in popular hits.
