IF you’re always looking to try out the latest skin care products that have got everyone talking, you’ll want to listen up. 

Beauty fans are currently rushing to Home Bargains to pick up two posh products that are scanning at the tills for just £1.99. 

Glossybox’s Deep Cleansing Creams and Gentle Foaming Cleansers are made by the beauty subscription company of the same name and usually retails for around £12.

But since they hit the shelves of Home Bargains, the product’s price has been slashed and the 100ml versions are priced at just £1.99.

And savvy shoppers are going wild for the duo, which come in pretty pink packaging and promise to “remove make-up and nourish skin”. 

One bargain hunter snapped a picture of the items on the well-stocked shelves, showing the impressive price tags too.
