The 2010 Grammy Awards: Full List of Nominees

July 2024 · 26 minute read

To the surprise of pretty much no one, Beyonce leads a jam-packed field of music hit machines with 10 nominations heading into the 2010 Grammys next month.

Taylor Swift is hot on her heels with eight nods, while her nemesis Kanye West, Maxwell and the Black Eyed Peas are all sitting pretty after scoring six apiece.

Beyonce is up for Album of the Year for her hot I Am… Sasha Fierce, Record of the Year for “Halo” and Song of the Year for “Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It).”

Not far behind was fellow Album of the Year nominee Swift, whose ’10 promises to be just as good as her ’09 after racking up eight noms thanks to Fearless.

Beyonce and Taylor Swift will duke it out at the Grammys next month. In terms of who wins more, that is. There will be no actual fighting involved. Sorry, Kanye.

Beyonce and Swift’s competition for top album? The Peas’ The E.N.D., Dave Matthews Band’s Big Whiskey and the GrooGrux King and Lady Gaga’s The Fame.

The 52nd Annual Grammy Awards will be handed out January 31 on CBS. Get the full (and rather long) list of Grammy Award nominations after the jump:
