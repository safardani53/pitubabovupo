June 2024 · 3 minute read

A MIDSIZE mum has hit back at haters after being told she looks "big" in clothes - insisting she's proud of her thich thighs and boobs.

Stephanie Vavron, from Aberdeen, regularly shares her fashion and makeup hauls on TikTok (@stephanievavron).

In a recent clip, the mum of two tried on a pair of leather trousers with a selection of tops from Boohoo.

But she was forced to shut down a troll after they told her "keep the top halves, the bottoms make you look really big".

Stephanie fumed: "I am not trying to look small, skinny, lean. That’s not my vibe.

"I want thick thighs. I’ve got thick thighs naturally, I’ve got a big ass, I’ve got big legs. I’ve got a cracking set of boobs.

"It’s taken me a long time to admit that, but my boobs are absolutely amazing when I’m not running on a treadmill 24/7 to keep myself as lean as possible and have abs."

She added: "Why when I try something on, I’m this really in between size. I’m not big and I’m not small. I’m just a natural average looking lassie size.

"Because of that, I cannot wear something without someone saying you look big, you look large, it’s doing nothing for your figure."

It's not the first time Stephanie has faced comments about her body, being told "you're so fat now" under another video.
