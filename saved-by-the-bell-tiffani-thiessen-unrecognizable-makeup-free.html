MORE ABOUT TIFFANI

Back in the late 80's early 90's, Tiffani played Kelly Kapowski on Saved By The Bell - alongside Mario Lopez, Mark-Paul Gosselaar, and Elizabeth Berkley.

The teen comedy series ran for four seasons from 1989 to 1993.

Before her big break, she was born on January 23, 1974, in Long Beach, California.

At first, she appeared in a commercial after her family encouraged her to take up acting when she was eight.

Along with SBTB, she appeared on other hit shows including Beverly Hills, 90210, White Collar, and Alexa & Katie.

From 2015 to 2017, Thiessen also hosted her own food-based television series called "Dinner at Tiffani's."

She is married to actor Brady Smith and they have two children: Harper, 13, and Holt, eight.
