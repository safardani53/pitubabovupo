What is a cross-fit game?

The CrossFit Games are the ultimate testing grounds for the Fittest Man and Fittest Woman on EarthTM and are recognized worldwide as the ultimate fitness test.

Fans worldwide have attended the multi-day sporting event, streamed the competition live, or watched it on ESPN, CBS, or several foreign broadcasts for the past 14 years.

Athletes from over 120 nations competed on the international stage.

The Worldwide Open, the world’s biggest participatory sporting event, is the starting point for the CrossFit Games.

One possibility is published online for the three-week competition every Thursday, and competitors have four days to record and submit scores.

Anyone over the age of 14 will register for the first stage of the CrossFit Games season, which includes categories for youth, age ranges, and adaptive athletes.

However, the 2021 year’s Open will be the most accessible in CrossFit history, with athletes having the choice of competing at a nearby gym or home with basic or no equipment.
