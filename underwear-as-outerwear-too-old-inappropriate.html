I love wearing my underwear as outerwear - I know I might be too old to pull it off and its inapp

August 2024 · 3 minute read

A WOMAN has revealed that she loves wearing racy lingerie as outerwear even if it isn't 'family appropriate'.

Madeleine White, who is from the UK but lives in the US, showed off her latest collecting of Savage X Fenty bodysuits that were so pretty she wanted to make sure people could see them.

In the clip she held up a purple lacy number as well as a black mesh body with blue floral detailing on the cups.

In a clip posted to social media, she said: "Let's style some more sexy lingerie as an outfit, it's always the most fun in the fall, because you can layer."

The fashion pro went for the black see-through underwear with exposed boning to wear out for the day.

The stunning bodysuit left little to the imagination when Madeleine put it on, so she decided she had to make it a little more 'family appropriate.'

She first tried it on with some low-rise oversized jeans which showed off her figure and created a cut-out detail around her hips.

But the fashion whizz decided she was a bit too old to show off that much flesh.

"This is really cute, but I feel like I'm too old for this, if I was 18, definitely. Yes," she added.

Instead, she opted for a maxi denim skirt which sat higher on her hips to give her more coverage.

The fashion pro paired the look with some knee-high black boots and decided to add a jacket on top.

She first tried a denim jacket which went perfectly with her lingerie, but didn't quite match the skirt.

Madeleine then tried a black blazer and a leather jacket, but neither went with the look.

Finally, she decided to brave it all and wear the lingerie piece without any other layers.

A silver shoulder bag finished the look off for Madeleine who was ready to get on with her day with her risky ensemble,

The clip of her styling her underwear as outerwear was clearly a hit with other fashion fans as the TikTok video posted to her account @madeleine_white went viral with over two million views.

Plenty of people took to the comments to share their thoughts on the outfit.

One person wrote: "The best outfit."

Another commented: "You’re never to old to wear what makes you happy!!!"

"This is so 2010s," penned a third.
