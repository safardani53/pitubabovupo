How can I buy the Shark Tank beef Wellingtons?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

BEEF Wellington took center stage on Shark Tank during the Friday, October 13, 2023 episode of the hit show.

Now, hungry fans want to know how they can get their hands on the food product.

What are the Shark Tank beef Wellingtons?

The Shark Tank beef Wellingtons are produced by the California-based business, Wellingtons.

Founders Arya Alexander and Anastasia Geroulis created the company in Los Angeles during the coronavirus pandemic.

Arya wrote on its website: "One fateful pandemic night in, my fiancé Anastasia and I were trying to solve one of the biggest mysteries plaguing modern couples - what the hell are we’re [sic] gonna eat for dinner?!

"Our stomachs were growling and our minds were racing as we played the kitchen drums for inspiration. Every recipe we came across was either too complicated or too boring. But, little did we know, we were on the brink of culinary greatness.

"Fast forward two years and we're still at it, making Wellingtons everyday [sic] in our commercial kitchen in Los Angeles.

"Only difference is we've brought on great partners like Pink Dot, Goldbelly, and Locale Market to help get our products to every door in the country. And we got married in June 2022."

On October 13, 2023, Shark Tank fans saw Arya and Anastasia appear on season 15, episode 3 of the popular TV series.

Many were displeased when none of the investors decided to make a deal with the two business owners despite speaking highly of their products.

How can I buy the Shark Tank beef Wellingtons?

Wellingtons has put a spin on traditional beef Wellington, selling it in multiple varieties, including:

  • Personal Beef Wellington - $35.00
  • Large Beef Wellington - $90.00
  • King Beef Wellington - $180.00
  • Breakfast Wellington - $13.00
  • Cheeseburger Wellington - $15.00
  • Cheeseburger Welly Bites (30 pieces) - $25.00
  • Nuttelington with Nutella (2 pack) - $15.00

It also sells two sauces, including Savage Style for $4.00 and Truffle Horseradish for $7.00.

The brand is now offering a Shark Tank Pack for $150.00, which includes:

  • Two Personal Beef Wellingtons
  • Two Cheeseburger Wellingtons w/ Jalapeños
  • Two Breakfast Wellingtons
  • Two Truffle Horseradish Cream Sauces
  • Two Savage Sauces
  • Free Cheeseburger Welly Bites (15 pieces)

Customers can purchase from Wellingtons via the company's website.

According to Tasting Table, its food items are also available through "major food shipping services," including Goldbelly nationwide and Uber Eats in Los Angeles.

How can I watch Shark Tank?

You can tune in to Shark Tank on Fridays at 8 pm ET on ABC.

The hit television show is also available for streaming on Hulu and fuboTV.
