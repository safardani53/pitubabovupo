Natasha Leggero - The Amazing Race Cast Member

September 2024 Â· 2 minute read
Natasha Leggero began performing on the Chicago stage when she was 10 years old. Since then, she has parlayed her talents into a successful, diverse career as an actor, producer, and writer.

In 2015, Leggero created, wrote, produced, and starred in the Comedy Central's Another Period, alongside Riki Lindhome, from 2015-2018. Additionally, she was featured in the Emmy-nominated web series Burning Love. Leggero starred in the comedy Free Agents and Dice. Her additional television credits include Community, Marry Me, It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, Arrested Development, The League, Reno 911!, Drunk History, Modern Family, Suburgatory, Key and Peele, Comedy Bang! Bang!, Are You There, Chelsea?, and The Tonight Show, among others. Also, she received notoriety as a "roaster," appearing on Comedy Central Roast of James Franco, Comedy Central Roast of Justin Bieber, and Netflix's Historical Roasts, which she did in character as Mary Todd Lincoln.

Leggero's voice can be heard on multiple animated series such as Ugly Americans, Brickleberry, The Life and Times of Tim, Aqua Teen Hunger Force, and China, IL. Also, Leggero is known for her tenure as a regular roundtable panelist on Chelsea Lately.

On the big screen, Leggero's credits include Neighbors alongside Seth Rogen, and Let's Be Cops opposite Jake Johnson and Damon Wayans, Jr. Also, she appeared in the 2009 romantic comedy He's Just Not That Into You.

In 2015, she starred in her first hour-long special, Live at Bimbo's for Comedy Central. Her Netflix special, The Honeymoon Stand Up Special, premiered in April of 2018 and subsequently paved the way for her growth into the digital space, as she now hosts the rapidly growing Endless Honeymoon podcast with husband, Moshe Kasher. Currently, she is writing a book of humorous parenting stories for Simon & Schuster.

Born and raised in Rockford, IL, Leggero moved to New York City after high school where she studied at the Stella Adler Conservatory and received her B.A. in theater criticism from Hunter College. Currently, she lives in Los Angeles with her husband and daughter. Her birthday is March 26. She can be followed on Twitter and Instagram @natashaleggero and on Facebook @NatashaLeggero.
