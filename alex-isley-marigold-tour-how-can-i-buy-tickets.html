Alex Isley Marigold tour: How can I buy tickets?

September 2024 · 2 minute read

MUSICAL artist Alex Isley announced she is going on tour in the summer of 2022.

She will be joined on tour by LAYA, Gwen Bunn, and Zyah Belle. 

When is Alex Isley's Marigold tour?

Alex Isley's Marigold tour kicks off in San Fransisco, California on August 10 and will extend across North America.

The tour will take place in 13 states and will wrap up on September 3 in Chicago, Illinois.

Isley announced the Marigold tour on her Instagram page on May 11 and wrote, "I’M GOING ON TOUR!! I cannot WAIT to see y’all!!!!"

The tour will promote her newest album, Marigold, which was released on March 30, 2022.

Marigold was a collaborative effort with Jack Dine and in an interview with R&B, Isley said, “It’s a lot of reflecting on this album.

“This album reflects memories — some pleasant and some not so pleasant.”

How can I buy tickets?

Fans looking to buy tickets to Alex Isley's Marigold tour can do so by visiting her website and selecting the tour tab.

Tickets start at between $15 and $20 depending on the location with the option to purchase early entry VIP tickets for $72 or the ultimate meet and greet and soundcheck VIP package for $147.

Those wishing to purchase tickets can also do so on Ticketmaster, Viagogo, and VividSeats.
