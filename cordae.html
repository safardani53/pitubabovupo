Who is Cordae?

Cordae Aamari Dunston mononymously known as Cordae formerly YBN Cordae and Entendre is an American Rapper, singer, and songwriter. He gained popularity through his remixes of songs such as My Name Is, by Eminem and Old Niggaz.

He amassed more than 2.3 million Instagram followers as a result of his social media success. Because of his unique and upbeat blend of hip-hop and trap music, he stands out in the music industry.

His fans love his original style and creative music production. He is gaining popularity and is a budding star. He is well-known in this era for his amazing rapping and singing abilities.

Cordae’s skill and exceptional performance have helped him build a solid reputation in the music and rapping industries. Because of him, they became well-known as one of the most important and talented rappers in American music history.
