Emmerdale bosses begged Sammy Winward to come back as Katie Sugden's ghost but star says she's GLAD

September 2024 Â· 2 minute read

SAMMY Windward says Emmerdale bosses begged her to come back to the soap as a ghost - but she turned them down because she was too busy.

The star, 33, played Katie Sugden on the ITV soap for 14 years before being murdered by her ex-lover in a dramatic love triangle twist.

Just a year after her character was killed off, Emmerdale bosses planned for her to appear to her husband Andy Sugden. Unfortunately, it wasn't meant to be.

She told Inside Soap: "About a year after I left Emmerdale, they asked me to return as a ghost!

"It was for a scene or two with Andy. I was working, so I couldn't fit it in. But I'd be up for a cheeky little ghost appearance now - if only Kelvin was still in Emmerdale!"

While fans have been clamouring for Katie to make a surprise return to the farm, Sammy is happy her character is gone forever so she has no choice but to stay away.

Emmerdale's Robert Sugden makes a plan to leave the village after pleading guilty to assault

The actress continued: "I get asked all the time when I'm coming back to Emmerdale, and I'm like, 'I'm dead!'

"But then again, Harold Bishop fell off a cliff in Neighbours and survived. I'm glad they killed Katie off - it meant it was more final for me. Now, I'm not always wondering if I should go back!"

Sammy played Katie from 2001 to 2015 before she was murdered by her ex-lover and brother-in-law Robert Sugden.

The pair had a steamy affair that threatened her relationship with eventual husband Andy Sugden (Kelvin Fletcher).
