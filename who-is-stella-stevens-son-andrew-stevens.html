ANDREW Stevens is the only son of the late Stella Stevens.

He is an American actor, producer, and director with some major blockbuster hits.

Who is Stella Stevens' son, Andrew Stevens?

Andrew Stevens is the CEO and President of Andrew Stevens Entertainment and Stevens Entertainment Group.

He has produced, directed, or financed more than 180 films throughout his career.

He is well known for his hit movie The Whole Nine Yards and its sequel, The Whole Ten Yards, and for the movie The In-Laws.

He started his career in acting, even trying out for the part of Luke Skywalker in the original Star Wars.

However, he soon moved on to the behind-the-scenes part of the film industry, which led to him running Franchise Pictures, which produced movies for Warner Brothers.

Stevens worked for Franchise Pictures from 1997 through 2002, when he started Stevens Entertainment Group.

He is also well-known in film schools across the country for publishing textbooks, including Foolproof Filmmaking, Producing for Profit, and Screenwriting for Profit.

In addition to the money made from the film industry, Stevens has also made some of his fortunes from real estate and as a restaurateur.

In addition to his fame, Stevens has also faced his share of hardships, including struggling with addiction in the 1990s.

He was able to overcome his addiction and has been sober for more than two decades now.

Does Andrew Stevens have children?

Stevens has been married several times, leaving many wondering whether he has children.

He has three children with his second wife, Robyn Suzanne Scott, but they all lead private lives.

Stevens' mother, the beloved actress Stella Stevens, died on February 17, 2023.

She was 84 years old and had been battling Alzheimer's disease.
