Who is Nessa Barrett? 

An American singer and songwriter, initially known as Janesa Jaisa Barrett, suddenly rose to fame due to her famous video on TikTok, a video-sharing app in 2019.

A dancing celeb and a pro lip sync artist, she has more than 17 million followers on her nessaabarrett account.

Her song “I hope you are miserable until you’re dead” was her first song to chart on US Billboard Hot 100 at number 88.

Early Life of Nessa Barrett

She was interested in music since her school days and holds artists like Lana Del Ray, Arctic Monkeys, and The Neighbourhood as her greatest influences.
