Chris Browns Accuser Once Posed for Cheesy Bikini Photo Shoot

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Oh, dear. Baylee Curran, the woman Chris Brown allegedly threatened with a gun, seems to have a saucy side. Photos taken in June of the former beauty queen posing in a bikini on a rock near Las Vegas have surfaced on the internet amid news of the rapper’s latest legal trouble.

In the glamour shots, the blonde — wearing a teeny gold string bikini — lies in the middle of the desert while attempting to pose seductively with her hair in her mouth. 

The dethroned Miss California Regional 2016 winner also posed wearing itty-bitty jean shorts while sitting on the side of a cliff.

As previously reported, Curran accused the “Loyal” rapper of pointing a gun at her after she admired a piece of jewelry at his house during a gathering on early Tuesday, August 30.

“I was scared for my life,” she told E! News of the alleged incident.

“Chris came in, pulled out his gun and said, ‘I’m getting so sick of you people,’ pointed the gun at me, [and told me] to get the eff out of the house,” she claimed to Extra.

After an hours-long standoff with police on Tuesday, Brown was arrested on suspicion of assault with a deadly weapon. He was released on $250,000 bail late Tuesday night and his lawyer, Mark Geragos, has since denied the allegations.

“Thanks to everyone for their support and well wishes. Chris is out and well. The allegations against him are demonstrably false #TeamBreezy,” Geragos tweeted on Wednesday, August 31.
