CELEBS Go Dating star Jessika Power has been churning cash since joining OnlyFans with posts dubbed "too graphic to describe".

The 29-year-old former Married At First Sight Australia bride has made £26,000 from her first five days on the platform alone.

According to the Daily Telegraph, a newspaper in Jessika's native Australia, she "leaves absolutely nothing to the imagination" with her posts.

It claimed one video was "too graphic to describe" and said that there was "a lot more than lingerie and bikini shots on display".

The star, a divisive figure on her days on lockdown hit MAFS Australia, has 9,000 followers on the site.

She recently admitted: "I wish I had started it a year ago because I would be rich."

Jessika is one of the stars appearing on the upcoming British series of Celebs Go Dating after splitting from her British boyfriend.

She broke up with Manchester-based podcaster Connor Thompson, - just eight days after relocating from Australia to be with him.
