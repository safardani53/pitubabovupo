Domina Season 2 Is Set To Premiere On Sky Atlantic

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Domina season 2, the highly anticipated second season of the renowned historical British-Italian drama series, will premiere with the first two episodes on Sky Atlantic on Sunday, July 9, 2023, at 2 a.m. GMT. The series was created and written by Simon Burke, and it was directed by David Evans, Claire McCarthy, and Debs Paterson.

Domina is an epic narrative about the strong wife of Roman Emperor Augustus Caesar, Livia Drusilla. On June 2, 2023, the official trailer for the series’ second season was published. Since then, fans of the show have been excitedly awaiting the arrival of the second season, particularly since the first season ended on such an exciting note, with Gaius and Livia putting aside their differences over Marcellus’ death. So, without further ado, let’s dive right in to learn all there is to know about Domina season 2 before it premieres on Sky Atlantic.

What is the entire schedule for the Sky Atlantic series’ second season’s eight episodes?

The highly awaited new season of the Sky Atlantic series, Domina, will feature eight episodes total and will premiere with episodes 1 and 2 on Sunday, July 9, 2023, at 2 a.m. GMT. Weekly new episodes will be published at the same time.

Take a look at the full schedule for all eight episodes, along with their names, below:

  • Episode 1: Conspiracy – Sunday, July 9, 2023
  • Episode 2: Wedding – Sunday, July 9, 2023
  • Episode 3: Betrayal – Sunday, July 16, 2023
  • Episode 4: Exile – Sunday, July 23, 2023
  • Episode 5: Sacrilege – Sunday, July 30, 2023
  • Episode 6: Freedom – Sunday, August 6, 2023
  • Episode 7: Curse – Sunday, August 13, 2023
  • Episode 8: Control – Sunday, August 13, 2023

What can we anticipate from the show’s second season?

The epic historical series’ latest season will continue with the big story of Rome’s ruling couple, Livia and Gaius. Season 2 will include a heated battle for dominance and authority over the whole Roman Empire and the Imperial Family of Rome. The tale will be told from a female perspective, as it was in season 1.

The opening episode of the second season will feature Gaius and Livia returning to Rome during a famine. Gaius will also launch a dramatic race to succession in this episode. The wedding ceremony of Antonina and Drusus is jeopardized in the second episode of the series’ new season, and Livia’s marriage is put to the test. Without a question, the audience is in for a tremendously engaging new season of Domina.

Who is on the cast list for the next season of the historical drama?

Season 2 of the series will include the following cast members:

  • Kasia Smutniak as Livia
  • Matthew McNulty as Gaius
  • Darrell D’Silva as Piso
  • Liah O’Prey as Young Julia
  • Claire Forlani as Octavia
  • Ben Batt as Agrippa
  • Ewan Horrocks as Young Drusus
  • Christine Bottomley as Scribonia
  • Alaïs Lawson as Young Marcella
