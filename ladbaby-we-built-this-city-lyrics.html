Has he written any other songs?

We Built This City is the first song that LadBaby has written.

His debut song was released on 14th December and within less than a week racked up almost 900,000 views.

The track was released in aid of poverty charity The Trussell Trust.

Bookies currently have him as a firm favourite to get to Christmas Number One with Ladbrokes giving him odds of 6/1 putting him just behind Ariana Grande.

Who is Ladbaby?

Mark Hoyle, 31, aka LadBaby is a blogger and YouTuber from Nottingham.

He previously worked as a graphic designer in London before he started to make parenting and budgeting videos.

He's also an entrepreneur and inventor of the toolbox lunchbox.

In 2018 he was crowned Celebrity Dad of the Year beating the likes of Prince Williamand Liam Payne.

Mark Hoyle has been married to his wife Roxanne since 2015.

She is also a blogger and YouTuber who goes by the name LadBabyMum.

She was previously an amateur UK free diver.

The couple have two sons Phoenix, two and Kobe, born in 2018.
