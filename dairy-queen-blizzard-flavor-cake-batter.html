Dairy Queen to launch new blizzard flavor in days fans are 'jumping behind the counter' over it

September 2024 · 3 minute read

FANS are excited about a new flavor that's coming to Dairy Queen.

One of the most popular offerings the fast food chain has is its blizzards.

Some of its varieties include twists on common household snacks including Oreos, Reese's, and Snickers.

But soon a new flavor known as cake batter cookie dough will be joining the Dairy Queen blizzard family, the company confirmed to The U.S. Sun.

A spokesperson from Dairy Queen described the new offering as having a: "delicious DQ soft serve, cake batter flavor, [and] confetti cookie dough pieces."

It added that they are all: "combined to deliver you the cake batter fix you never knew you needed."

The new item will launch on June 26 in participating US locations.

It's unclear if it will be available only for a limited time – but it's a part of Dairy Queen's July Blizzard of the Month offering.

FAN REACTIONS

The confirmation comes after food snack account on Instagram @snackolator revealed from a local manager that the cake batter cookie dough would be coming in June.

Fans in the comments were filled with euphoria.

One said in response to the cake batter cookie dough blizzard: "Yeap ! I’m jumping behind the counter."

Also, some fans were reminiscing about a similar popular flavor that was discontinued by Dairy Queen known as confetti cake.

A cautiously optimistic commenter wrote: "it was sooooo good, like wayyy too good. Hopefully they bring it back, or hopefully this is just as good!

First introduced in 2012, the confetti cake was brought back briefly in 2014 – but hasn't been on the menu since.

It came with cake pieces, soft serve ice cream, and confetti frosting.

Also, keep in mind that McDonald's has launched a confetti cookie dough McFlurry.

MORE ON DAIRY QUEEN

The new menu option that will launch comes as Dairy Queen has implemented other menu changes.

This includes options for the spring and the summer, such as the Oreo Brookie Blizzard and Peanut Butter Puppy Chow Blizzard.

Another includes bringing back a fan-favorite shake: the Mint Brownie Blizzard.

However, keep in mind that these three are only available at select locations nationwide and will remain on the menu for just a limited time.

Meanwhile, Dairy Queen confirmed to The U.S. Sun that a location in Carbondale, Illinois will be closing.
