29 cities and states sending payments between $200 and $1,200 monthly - full list of universal basic

June 2024 · 5 minute read

AMERICANS all over the country are getting help making ends meet through universal basic income (UBI) programs.

These guaranteed income programs provide monthly payments to eligible participants. 

Usually, the programs are government-sponsored or run by not-for-profits.

In most cases, there are no strings attached to how the cash can be used and it doesn't have to be paid back.

While there is growing support or these programs, those who oppose them say they discourage people from finding work.

Here are some of the UBI programs across the country.
