Amy Duggar shows off cute Jurassic Park theme family costumes with husband Dillon &amp; son Daxton, two

March 2024 · 4 minute read

AMY Duggar showed off her adorable Jurassic Park-themed family costumes with her husband Dillon & their son Daxton.

The former reality star shared the group pics on her Instagram, mimicking characters from the iconic science-fiction film.

The couple played the parts of paleontologist Alan Grant and Ellie Sattler in the film, portrayed by actors Sam Neill and Laura Dern.

Amy, 35, gave a spot-on impression of her character sporting tan shorts, a pink button down tied at the front and brown boots.

She pulled her hair back into a loose ponytail to top off the look.

Her husband Dillon opted for brown shorts, a jean button down, a wide-brimmed hat and red hankerchief tied around his neck.

The pair's adorable two-year-old son Dax joined in on the fun donning a full-body dinosaur suit.

The trio took photos in a wooded area surrounded by trees to set the scene.

In one snap, all three are huddled together as the toddler held his parent's hands with the costume covering his face.

Another snap gave a close-up shot of the tot taking a breather from beneath the dinosaur head.

A third pic showed the father-son duo posing for the camera wearing the full outfit.

The parents-of-one took another snapshot kneeling down next to their son as the toddler opened his mouth real wide imitating the dinosaur roar.

Amy captioned the post: "Welcome to Jurassic Park #halloween2021"

Fans commented on the pics in awe of the family's creative costumes, with one writing: "Soo stinkin cute!"

While another wrote: "Adorable!! Look at that T. rex!!"

THROWBACK HALLOWEEN

Over the weekend, Amy shared throwback pics of her and her husband donning much riskier outfits for Halloween.

In the snapshot, the Counting On alum was dressed up as the prostitute Vivian Ward (Julia Roberts) from Pretty Woman.

Just like in the sexy romantic comedy, the TLC star rocked a tight white top, which teased her cleavage and midriff. 

She wore knee-high boots and a red sweater over her blue skirt. 

Amy captioned the photo: "So what happened after he climbed up to the tower and rescued her?"

The ex TV star added: "She rescues him right back" with a kiss emoji.  

The flashback photo was taken when Amy and Dillon - who was dressed up as Richard Gere's playboy Edward Lewis - had gone to Sin City for Halloween back in 2018.  

In the Instagram story, the TV personality said she "got the fairytale" in a "Vegas style."

Amy poked fun in her post including the hashtag "#forthefundies" which is a reference to fundamentalists and their strict interpretation of scripture.

STRICT RULES

Amy has broken her Christian family’s strict rules set in place by patriarch and matriarch Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar.

The Duggar family observed a strict dress code that usually only permits women to dress modestly and to wear skirts or dresses.

One of these inflexible rules includes not allowing women to show their legs or dress in a revealing manner.

SECOND BIRTHDAY PARTY

Last month, the couple celebrated their son's second birthday with a homemade two-tier cake and a toddler-sized jeep for the little one.

Amy took to her social media page to share behind-the-scenes snaps from her young son's birthday, which didn't appear to have any Duggar guests.

"2nd birthday party was a hit! This little guy is TWO cool," she tagged the snaps, which showed off Dax's miniature Jeep as he drove in the garden.

The tiny blue car had its own personalized number plate, "Daxs Ride" - and the tot drove around in a backwards cap decorated with the word "two".

Inside Amy and Dillon's house, a play area had been set up for Dax and his friends to climb on - while a cake was placed on the kitchen counter.
