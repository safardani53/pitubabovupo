TRAVIS Barker's daughter Alabama has dropped a joke about her dating life in new TikTok while celebrating her 18th birthday.

Alabama has posted a TikTok announcing she's ready to explore the dating scene in a new way now that she's 18.

The young socialite celebrated her milestone birthday on December 24.

As Alabama turned the corner into adulthood, she made a bold announcement that stunned some followers.

She took to TikTok wearing a strapless lace dress.

In the video, Alabama wore her long platinum blond hair down in loose curls.
