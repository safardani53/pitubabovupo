CASH-SAVVY foodies have revealed where you can get your hands on a massive Christmas feast for £15 - and it will feed four hungry guests.

The festive season comes with a lot of joy and wonderful memories - but for many cash-strapped families across the nation Christmas also arrives with financial worries.

As the supermarket prices are skyrocketing, millions of households in the UK may wonder if it's possible to prepare a mouth-watering feast without forking out a fortune - and turns out, it is.

According to food fans, there is a less-known retailer that now offers a bargain deal in time for the pricey December - and you can feed four mouths for undere £20.

After a major success last year, the supermarket, Heron Foods, is back this season too with everything you may need for the feast table.

Bargain-hunting shoppers can now snap up some of the cult favourite festive treats on the cheap, including a massive bag of Glorious Duck Fat Roast Potatoes and a four-pack of gigantic Yorkshire Puddings by Aunt Bessie's.
