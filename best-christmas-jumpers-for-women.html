    Where to buy Christmas jumpers for women

    You can find a fab selection of the best Christmas jumpers for women online at stores such as Amazon, Very, Marks & Spencer and ASOS.

    High street stores, too, offer a great range if you’re out and about – try John Lewis, New Look and River Island for starters.

    Don’t forget the big supermarkets also, as you can often pick up a Christmas jumper or two while you’re doing your weekly shop – Sainsbury’s, Asda and Tesco all include Christmas jumpers and sweatshirts among their festive offerings.

    When is Christmas Jumper Day 2022?

    Save the Children's Christmas Jumper Day falls on the 8th December this year.

    You're encouraged to wear a silly jumper and donate £2 towards this worthy cause.

    Remember to use the hashtag #christmasjumperday on social media and show off your festive attire.

    How should I wear a Christmas jumper?

    However you like!
    There are no rules when it comes to Christmas jumpers - chuck it on with a pair of jeans, or go full-out with a santa hat and festive trousers.
